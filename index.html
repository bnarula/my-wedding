DIY Photo Frame - Reuse the Wedding Card

For those of us, who wants to do some DIY, we can reuse the wedding card and create a table photo frame from it. That is the idea I had in my mind (A less creative one, I know.. ) You can come up with your own DIY, or maybe check out what I did with the card.
Please do this only after my wedding is done ;P I did it only for Demo.

  1. Use this site : PhotoCollage to create a photo collage from your memories.

  2. Use the custom canvas size as 925X375

    .. this will make sure that the aspect ratio is as per the wedding card.

  3. Add one or more images to create your own style of Collage.

  4. Get the printout of the image on A4 sheet (Potrait Mode), with the image in the centre.

    .. a color printout might look better, but black&white will work as well.

  5. Now, this is all we are going to need here.

    .. a pair of scissors missing in the pic will also be required.

  6. Fold the paper according to the card size from top and bottom

    .. do not cut the paper from top and bottom, only trim it from the left and right side.

  7. Folding of the paper should be done like this.

  8. so that it fits the card like this.

    .. we are doing this to form a proper base of the photo-frame.

  9. This is our target:

  10. Put the glue on the front surface of the card and the back side of the paper.

  11. Paste the glue at the bottom edge of the paper. This is will go and stick to the elevated back side of the card. While the middle edge will be the base.

    .. I hope you are getting what I am trying here, I am no DIY Expert :)

  12. Make sure you firmly stick the paper to the card. This is slight tricky.

  13. We are getting close:

  14. The top edge goes behind the card and is stuck by glue.

  15. This is the outcome:

    .. its good to have your loved ones always around the table.

  16. I hope you liked it. You can try your own creativity of course. If possible, please do not throw away the card in the bin, have some DIY FUN.
